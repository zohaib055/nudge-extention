What is a Nothing?

A Nothing is a currency you earn when you close a site after being nudged.

Every Nothing stands for a time that you quit an addictive site.

They're called Nothings because they are worth nothing.

But that doesn't mean they don't matter. Having a lot of Nothings means a lot of extra moments in your life kissing goodbye to crappy stuff and saying hello to slightly better stuff.

Because they are worth nothing, you can spend Nothings on things that money can't buy (cheesy, huh?).

Spend them on being farther from your computer, or closer to family, or switching to a site that you enjoy more, or planning dinner with friends, or doing exercise, or watching a favorite TV show.

Nothings aren't about getting you to work harder — they're about helping you spend time in a better way.
